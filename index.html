What's 5 More?


5 More is a progressive overload workout web app that is built to improve performance and give feedback based on each user's unique input.


We will ask a couple of questions like:


What exercice are you doing?


How many reps?


How many sets?


How much weight?


Then you tell us if you actually completed this workout and we'll run the reults through 1 of 2 algorithms based off if you successfully completed the workout or failed it.


The site will tell you wether you should go up in x amount of weight next week or even back down.
